OUR TEAM

STAFF

Lion Cai

Chair

Lion Cai is a 25-year-old chairperson of the charity organization Music Can Change The World. Despite his young age, Lion is already making a big impact in the world of music and charity. His organization aims to help underprivileged children learn about music and develop their talents, with a focus on bringing positive change to their lives and communities. Lion's passion for music started at a young age, and he quickly realized the transformative power it can have on people's lives. He founded Music Can Change The World to share this passion and help children from disadvantaged backgrounds discover the joy and benefits of music.

Tullly Russell

Deputy Chair

At just 27 years old, Tully Russell is already making waves in the world of music and charity as the deputy chair of Music Can Change The World. Tully's passion for music began at a young age, and he quickly realized that it could be a powerful tool for positive change. He joined Music Can Change The World to help underprivileged children discover the transformative power of music. As deputy chair, Tully is responsible for ensuring that the organization runs smoothly and effectively.
